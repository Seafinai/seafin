Frequently Asked

NOTE_01 What types of businesses do you work with? +
We specialize in small to medium-sized businesses across all industries. Our solutions scale from solo operators to teams of 50+, and we tailor every implementation to your specific industry and workflow needs.
NOTE_02 How long does a custom AI agent project take? +
Most projects complete in 4 to 8 weeks from discovery to deployment. Complex integrations may extend this timeline, but we always provide clear milestones and weekly progress updates so you know exactly where things stand.
NOTE_03 Do I need technical expertise on my team? +
Not at all. We manage the full technical stack. Every solution includes intuitive interfaces, thorough documentation, and training sessions so your team can operate confidently without writing a single line of code.
NOTE_04 What is Custodian Backup? +
Custodian Backup is our managed care tier. Starting at $9 per month, it provides automated monitoring, encrypted daily backups, and priority support. Higher tiers add dedicated account management and guaranteed response times.
NOTE_05 Can you integrate with our existing tools? +
Absolutely. We have experience integrating with over 15 platforms including Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Shopify, Google Workspace, and many more. During discovery, we map your entire tool ecosystem and plan seamless connections.
NOTE_06 What happens after the project launches? +
We remain your partner. Post-launch support includes monitoring, optimization, and iterative improvements. Our optional Custodian Backup plans ensure your systems stay healthy and evolve with your business over time.
