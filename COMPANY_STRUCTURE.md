# Seafin LLC — Company Structure

## Organization Chart

```
Seafin LLC (legal entity)
│
├── Seafin (services brand)
│   ├── BUILD — Custom AI agents, KnowledgeClaw, SaaS dev, AI websites
│   ├── AUTOMATE — WorkClaw, document processing, AI analytics
│   ├── CONNECT — SupportClaw, ChatClaw, VoiceClaw, ContentClaw (OpenClaw platform)
│   └── PROTECT — Custodian managed backup ($9-199/mo)
│
└── Solvity (product brand)
    ├── Self-Service Platform — 37+ pre-built AI skills ($59-299/mo)
    ├── Custom Solutions — Tailored automations (delivered by Seafin consulting team)
    └── Future Products — Additional SaaS tools under the Solvity brand
```
