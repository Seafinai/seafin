FAQ

Frequently Asked

01 Who is Seafin.ai built for? +
Companies with 25-500 employees. Repeatable workflows. Some technical capacity. Need custom solutions, not generic SaaS. Spending 10+ hours/week on work that should be automated? We can help.
02 How long does a project take? +
From first call to deployment: 2-6 weeks for quick wins (n8n, RAG), 6-12 weeks for comprehensive solutions. Includes discovery, scoping, proposal (1-2 weeks), then development with weekly demos.
03 Is my data secure? +
Yes. Enterprise-grade security with encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, and audit logging. Cloud APIs are governed by provider security terms (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google). For complete data sovereignty or air-gapped environments, we offer local model deployment on your infrastructure.
04 Will it work with our existing tools? +
Almost certainly. We integrate with Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Shopify, Google Workspace, and many more. During discovery we map your full tool stack and plan seamless connections.
05 What happens after launch? +
Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support and optimization. Ongoing hosting costs $10-200/mo (infrastructure + API usage). Full monitoring, updates, and support included — we handle everything so it stays running 24/7.
06 What AI models do you support? +
Cloud APIs (Standard): Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT, Google Gemini, Moonshot Kimi — Fastest performance, easiest deployment, fully managed by us.

Local Models (Enterprise): Kimi K2.5, Qwen, Llama — For air-gapped or high-security environments. Requires enterprise hardware (48GB+ GPU VRAM, server-grade infrastructure). Complete data sovereignty.

Most customers use cloud APIs for optimal performance and simplicity.
07 What does it cost? +
Project costs vary based on complexity and scope:

Quick Wins (1-4 weeks): $2,500-$7,500 — Simple n8n workflows, basic RAG bots, focused automation tools

Standard Projects (4-8 weeks): $7,500-$17,500 — Custom AI assistants, multi-system integrations, comprehensive automation

Enterprise Solutions (8-12 weeks): $17,500+ — Complex workflows, custom models, full-scale implementations

All projects include: discovery, development, testing, deployment, 30 days post-launch support. Ongoing hosting/maintenance: $10-200/mo. We'll provide exact pricing during your strategy call based on your specific needs.
